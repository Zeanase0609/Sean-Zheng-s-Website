Character Design

Character design is the process of creating a character's appearance, personality, and backstory. It involves sketching, refining, and finalizing the character's look.

Pixel Art

Pixel art is a form of digital art where images are created using small square pixels. It is often used in video games and retro-style graphics.

Background Creation

Background creation involves designing the environment in which characters exist. It includes creating landscapes, buildings, and other elements that set the scene.
